# Drug Classification — Intelligent Predictive Modeling for Healthcare AI

In the medical field, prescribing the correct medication tailored to an individual patient is a critical task. Traditional manual prescription methods often rely heavily on complex clinical guidelines, which can lead to human error, delayed treatments, and potential adverse drug reactions due to oversight of patient demographics and lab results.

To address this challenge, we developed an intelligent Drug Classification System using Machine Learning to automate and optimize the drug selection process based on unique patient profiles with high precision.

Our goal was to build a reliable and scalable predictive model that assists healthcare professionals in decision-making, ensuring patients receive the safest and most effective drug type instantly, thereby lowering clinical operational risks and improving patient outcomes.

What makes this project unique is its structured data workflow, using advanced Feature Engineering and classification algorithms to handle mixed categorical and numerical medical data with optimal accuracy.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Machine Learning Frameworks:** Scikit-Learn
* **Classification Algorithms:** Decision Trees, Random Forests, or Logistic Regression (depending on the final model used)
* **Data Preprocessing & Encoding:** LabelEncoder, OneHotEncoder, and Feature Scaling
* **Data Analysis & Engineering:** Pandas & NumPy
* **Data Visualization:** Matplotlib & Seaborn
